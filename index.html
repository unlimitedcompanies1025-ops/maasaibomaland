About Maasai Boma Land

Maasai Boma Land is a cultural and tourism organization located in Tanzania, dedicated to preserving, promoting and sharing the authentic Maasai way of life with the world. We showcase the rich traditions, values and daily life of the Maasai people through dances, storytelling, ceremonies, traditional crafts and village experiences.

We are well known for displaying and selling genuine Maasai traditional products such as necklaces, beaded belts, sticks, spears, maces and many other handmade cultural items. Our visitors can also experience traditional Maasai drinks like Ormarwa, Engotoroki and Orbusa, which are prepared using ancient Maasai methods.

Our organization also educates visitors about important Maasai ceremonies such as male circumcision, female rites of passage, marriage traditions and the leadership system including the roles of Olaigwanani and Laiboni. We explain how different age sets are formed and how responsibilities are shared within the Maasai community.

Our Ownership

Maasai Boma Land is owned by the Maasai community as a cultural enterprise partnership. The community members are the founders, managers and caretakers of the business. As the organization grows, more youth, women and elders will be employed to strengthen the sustainability of the enterprise.
