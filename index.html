About Maasai Boma Land

Maasai Boma Land is known for dancing, making and selling different Maasai traditional tools such as necklaces, sticks, necklace belts, maces, and traditional drinks like Ormarwa, Engotoroki and Orbusa. We also showcase Maasai ceremonies including circumcision rites and traditional leadership systems such as Olaigwanani and Laiboniand also Maasai boma land is controlled and monitored by Maasai community in the representation mode of elected leaders who involve experts and other non-expert for representation of several departments like dancing and production department.

Company Summary

Maasai Boma Land is a Maasai cultural enterprise owned by the Maasai community. The business focuses on cultural tourism, village experiences and traditional products for visitors from all over the world.
