About Maasai Land Boma

Maasai Land Boma is a cultural owned by the Maasai community. We preserve, celebrate and share Maasai traditions through dances, ceremonies, leadership systems and the sale of authentic handmade products.

What We Do

We perform Maasai dances, show ceremonies like circumcision rites, leadership systems such as Olaigwanani and Laiboni, and sell necklaces, sticks, beads, mace and traditional drinks like Ormarwa, Engotoroki and Orbusa.

Ownership

Maasai Land Boma is owned by the Maasai community as a partnership organization. The partners are both owners and managers, ensuring culture and traditions are preserved.

Vision

To be number one in supplying Maasai traditional products and services in Tanzania and East Africa.

Mission

To improve people's lives by creating employment opportunities for women, youths, adults and elders in the Maasai community.

Goals

To become the most trusted Maasai organization in Arusha within 3 years and provide quality products at fair prices.

Objectives

To attract 2500 tourists by 2027, earn $5000 yearly and create employment for over 180 Maasai people.
