About Maasai Boma Land

Maasai Boma Land is a cultural and tourism organization located in Tanzania dedicated to preserving, promoting and sharing the authentic Maasai way of life through dances, ceremonies, crafts and village experiences.

We showcase Maasai traditional products such as necklaces, beaded belts, spears, sticks, maces and traditional drinks like Ormarwa, Engotoroki and Orbusa. We also educate visitors about Maasai leadership systems, age sets and important ceremonies.

Ownership

Owned by Maasai community as a cultural enterprise partnership.

Vision

To be number one supplier of Maasai products and services in Tanzania and East Africa.

Mission

To improve lives by creating jobs for youth, women and elders.

Goals

To become most trusted Maasai cultural enterprise in Arusha region.
